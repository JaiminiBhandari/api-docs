## Overview

Depositing funds into your ARP Digital account allows you to add money for trading, remittances, and other financial activities. We support multiple deposit methods for your convenience.

## Deposit Methods

### Bank Transfer

#### Supported Banks
- Major international banks
- Local banking networks in 190+ countries
- SWIFT wire transfers

#### How to Deposit via Bank Transfer

1. **Initiate Deposit**
   - Log in to your dashboard
   - Click "Deposit" in the sidebar
   - Select "Bank Transfer"

2. **Choose Currency and Amount**
   - Select deposit currency
   - Enter deposit amount
   - Review fees and processing time

3. **Get Deposit Details**
   - Receive bank account details
   - Account number, routing number, SWIFT code
   - Reference number for tracking

4. **Complete Transfer**
   - Transfer funds from your bank
   - Include reference number
   - Processing time: 1-3 business days

#### Bank Transfer Fees

| Amount Range | Fee | Processing Time |
|--------------|-----|-----------------|
| $0 - $1,000 | $5.00 | 1-2 business days |
| $1,001 - $10,000 | $10.00 | 1-2 business days |
| $10,001+ | $25.00 | 1-3 business days |

### Credit/Debit Card

#### Supported Cards
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- International cards accepted
- 3D Secure authentication required

#### How to Deposit via Card

1. **Select Card Deposit**
   - Choose "Credit/Debit Card" option
   - Enter deposit amount

2. **Enter Card Details**
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVV code
   - Cardholder name

3. **Complete Payment**
   - 3D Secure verification
   - Instant processing
   - Funds available immediately

#### Card Deposit Limits

| User Level | Daily Limit | Monthly Limit |
|------------|-------------|---------------|
| Basic | $1,000 | $5,000 |
| Verified | $5,000 | $25,000 |
| Premium | $10,000 | $50,000 |

### Cryptocurrency Deposits

#### Supported Cryptocurrencies
- Bitcoin (BTC)
- Ethereum (ETH)
- Tether (USDT)
- And 50+ other cryptocurrencies

#### How to Deposit Crypto

1. **Select Cryptocurrency**
   - Choose crypto deposit option
   - Select cryptocurrency

2. **Get Deposit Address**
   - Receive unique wallet address
   - QR code for mobile wallets

3. **Send Cryptocurrency**
   - Transfer from your wallet
   - Include transaction hash for tracking
   - Confirmations required: 1-6 (depending on crypto)

#### Crypto Deposit Fees

- **Network Fees**: Variable (paid by sender)
- **Platform Fee**: 0.1% of deposit amount
- **Processing Time**: 10-60 minutes after confirmations

## Deposit Tracking

### Deposit Status

Track your deposits in real-time:

- **Pending**: Deposit initiated
- **Processing**: Funds received, being verified
- **Completed**: Funds available in account
- **Failed**: Deposit rejected or returned
