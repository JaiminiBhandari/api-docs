## Overview

Your account balance represents the total balance available in your ARP Digital wallet. This includes fiat currencies and crypto currencies that you can use for transactions, trading, and withdrawals.

## Viewing Your Balance

### Dashboard Balance Overview

1. Log in to your [ARP Digital Dashboard](https://platform.arpdigital.io)
2. Your total balance is displayed prominently on the main dashboard
3. Click on "Balance" in the sidebar for detailed breakdown

## Balance Alerts

Set up balance alerts to stay informed:

### Low Balance Alert

- Get notified when your balance falls below a threshold
- Set minimum balance requirements

### High Balance Alert

- Monitor large balance increases
- Useful for security monitoring

### Currency-Specific Alerts

- Set alerts for specific currencies
- Track crypto price movements

## Balance Security

### Multi-Signature Protection

- All balance transfers require multiple approvals
- Enhanced security for large amounts

### Cold Storage

- High-value balances stored in offline cold storage
- Protected from online threats

### Insurance Coverage

- Balances up to $100,000 insured against platform risks
- Additional coverage available for higher amounts
