## Overview

Withdrawing funds from your ARP Digital account allows you to transfer money back to your bank account, crypto wallet, or other external accounts. We support multiple withdrawal methods with competitive fees and fast processing.

## Withdrawal Methods

### Bank Transfer Withdrawal

#### Supported Banks
- International wire transfers
- Local bank transfers in supported countries
- SWIFT network coverage

#### How to Withdraw via Bank Transfer

1. **Initiate Withdrawal**
   - Log in to dashboard
   - Click "Withdraw" in sidebar
   - Select "Bank Transfer"

2. **Select Withdrawal Account**
   - Choose from saved bank accounts
   - Or add new bank account

3. **Enter Withdrawal Details**
   - Select currency
   - Enter amount
   - Review fees and processing time

4. **Confirm Withdrawal**
   - Verify account details
   - 2FA confirmation required
   - Submit withdrawal request

#### Bank Transfer Fees

| Amount Range | Fee | Processing Time |
|--------------|-----|-----------------|
| $0 - $1,000 | $5.00 | 1-3 business days |
| $1,001 - $10,000 | $15.00 | 1-3 business days |
| $10,001+ | $35.00 | 2-5 business days |

### Cryptocurrency Withdrawal

#### Supported Cryptocurrencies
- Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT
- 50+ additional cryptocurrencies
- Cross-chain withdrawals supported

#### How to Withdraw Crypto

1. **Select Crypto Withdrawal**
   - Choose cryptocurrency
   - Enter withdrawal amount

2. **Provide Wallet Address**
   - Enter destination wallet address
   - Verify address format
   - Add memo/tag if required

3. **Confirm Transaction**
   - Review withdrawal details
   - 2FA verification
   - Submit withdrawal

#### Crypto Withdrawal Fees

- **Network Fee**: Variable (market rate)
- **Platform Fee**: 0.1% of withdrawal amount
- **Processing Time**: 10-60 minutes

### Withdrawal Status

Track withdrawals in real-time:

- **Pending**: Withdrawal initiated
- **Processing**: Funds sent, awaiting confirmation
- **Completed**: Funds received by recipient
- **Failed**: Withdrawal rejected or returned
