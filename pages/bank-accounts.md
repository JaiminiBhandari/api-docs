## Adding Bank Accounts

### Verification Process

1. **Account Information**
   - Bank name and address
   - Account number and routing code
   - Account holder name
   - Currency and account type

2. **Document Verification**
   - Bank statement (last 3 months)
   - Account ownership proof
   - Address verification
   - Identity documents

3. **Security Verification**
   - Micro-deposits ($0.01-0.99)
   - Instant verification (where available)
   - Manual review for large accounts

### Adding New Account

1. **Access Bank Accounts**
   - Log in to dashboard
   - Click "Bank Accounts" in sidebar

2. **Add Account**
   - Select "Add New Account"
   - Choose bank from list or manual entry

3. **Enter Details**
   - Account information
   - Upload verification documents
   - Submit for review

4. **Verification**
   - Micro-deposit verification
   - Document review (1-3 business days)
   - Account activation
