## Need an Account? Create One for Registration Setup

If you're new to ARP Digital, you'll need to create a Individual or Business account to access our dashboard. Follow these steps to get started:

#### Step 1: Visit the Registration Page

Go to [https://platform.arpdigital.io/register](https://platform.arpdigital.io/register) to begin the registration process.

#### Step 2: Create your account

Fill out the registration form with your personal details:

- **First Name**: Enter your first name.
- **Last Name**: Enter your last name.
- **Email**: Enter your valid email. 

#### Step 3: Create Account Credentials

Choose a strong password for your account:

- Use at least 8+ characters
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include at least one number
- Include at least one special symbol (!@#$%^&*)

#### Step 4: OTP Verify

- We sent a 6-digit code to your mail address. 
- After that vefify & continue.

#### Step 5: Finish setup

Set up your workspace to get started.

- Select one workspace :

    1. Individual

    2. Business
- Enter your Workspace name and create your workspace.

## Already Have an Account? Sign In and Login

If you already have an ARP Digital account, follow these steps to sign in:

#### Step 1: Visit the Login Page

Go to [https://platform.arpdigital.io/login](https://platform.arpdigital.io/login).

#### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

- **Email**: Enter the email address associated with your account
- **Password**: Enter your account password

<!--### Step 3: Two-Factor Authentication (if enabled)

If you have 2FA enabled, enter the verification code from your authenticator app or SMS.-->

#### Step 3: Access Your Dashboard

After successful login, you'll be redirected to your dashboard where you can:

- View your account overview
- Monitor transactions
- Manage API keys
- Configure webhooks

#### Forgot Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to set a new password

**Security Note**: This link can only be used once and will expire after 60 minutes.

### Next Steps

Once you're logged in to your dashboard:

1. **Generate API Keys**: Navigate to the API Keys section to create your sandbox and production credentials
2. **Test Integration**: Use our sandbox environment to test your integration
